Introducing Rebekah Hendricks!

I am a multimedia journalist specializing in audio and photo. I have a background in local news reporting, national polling reporting, and music reporting.

Here are my favorite journalism stories:

  1. Serial Season 1 by Sarah Koenig
    This was my introduction to investigative audio journalism. I fell in love with the medium though this podcast. I find that this is a great place to spark interest in reporting in those who have never engaged with journalism before.
  2. Math Or Magic? by This American Life
    This episode of This American Life reframed by vision of what arts and culture reporting can look like. I am very interested in small, maybe mundane stories, that resonate with people and the human experince.
  3. Why Fish Don't Exist by Lulu Miller
    This piece of science reporting mixed with personal memoir made me very interested in the world of science journalism and its possibilities. This book is at once a recounting of scientific history and a foundation of a philosophy on life.
